PM Vishwakarma Yojana

PM Vishwakarma Yojana aims to support traditional artisans and craftspeople by providing financial assistance, skill training, and modern tools. The scheme offers a subsidy on loans up to ₹1 lakh (in the first tranche) and ₹2 lakh (in the second tranche), with a concessional interest rate of 5%.

Age Limit:

18 years and above

How to Apply:

Eligible applicants can apply through Common Service Centers (CSCs) or the official PM Vishwakarma Yojana portal.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhar card
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Proof of age
  • Bank account details
  • Business details

Role to Apply:

Artisans, craftsmen, and traditional workers engaged in traditional crafts can apply.
