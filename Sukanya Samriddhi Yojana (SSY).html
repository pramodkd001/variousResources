Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

SSY is a small savings scheme launched to secure the future of the girl child. It offers a high-interest rate, with tax benefits. The account can be opened with a minimum deposit, and funds can be withdrawn for the girl's education or marriage once she turns 18.

Age Limit:

Girls aged below 10 years

How to Apply:

Visit any post office or authorized bank branch with the required documents.

Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate of the girl
  • Aadhar card of parents/guardian
  • Passport-sized photographs

Role to Apply:

Parent or legal guardian can open the account in the name of the girl child.
