Mudra Yojana for Women

Mudra Yojana offers loans up to ₹10 lakh without collateral, under three categories—Shishu, Kishor, and Tarun—based on the stage of the business. Women entrepreneurs benefit from lower interest rates and are encouraged to start small businesses like sewing, parlour services, or other self-employment ventures.

Age Limit:

No specific age limit

How to Apply:

Applications can be made through banks and financial institutions offering Mudra loans (under Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana).

Required Documents:

  • Business plan
  • Identity proof (Aadhar card, PAN card)
  • Bank account details
  • Proof of residence
  • Registration documents for the business (if any)

Role to Apply:

Women entrepreneurs looking to start or expand small businesses like tailoring shops, beauty parlours, or handicraft units can apply.
