Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

This maternity benefit program provides financial assistance of â‚¹5,000 to pregnant women for the first living child to help with proper nutrition and safe delivery.

Age Limit:

Pregnant women and lactating mothers, aged 19 years and above

How to Apply:

Apply through an Anganwadi center, authorized health facility, or online through the PMMVY website.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhar card
  • Bank passbook
  • MCP (Mother and Child Protection) card
  • ID proof

Role to Apply:

Eligible women, self-apply or through a health worker.
