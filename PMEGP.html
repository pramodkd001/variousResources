Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

PMEGP provides a subsidy of 15-35% on project costs to support small enterprises. It aims to generate employment opportunities by helping individuals set up micro-enterprises in non-farm sectors.

Age Limit:

No specific age limit

How to Apply:

Applications can be made through the official PMEGP website or at KVIC, KVIB, or District Industries Centres.

Required Documents:

  • Project report
  • Identity proof (Aadhar card, PAN card)
  • Bank account details
  • Proof of residence
  • Educational qualification certificates

Role to Apply:

Entrepreneurs who want to start a new micro-enterprise or expand an existing small business can apply.
