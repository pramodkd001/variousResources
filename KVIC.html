Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) Subsidy Scheme

The KVIC Subsidy Scheme aims to support the growth of Khadi and village industries by providing financial assistance in the form of subsidies on loans, helping entrepreneurs start or expand small-scale industries.

Age Limit:

No specific age limit

How to Apply:

Applications can be submitted through KVIC offices or banks affiliated with the KVIC Subsidy Scheme.

Required Documents:

  • Project report
  • Identity proof (Aadhar card)
  • Bank account details
  • Proof of residence
  • Business registration documents (if applicable)

Role to Apply:

Entrepreneurs looking to start or expand Khadi or village industries can apply.
