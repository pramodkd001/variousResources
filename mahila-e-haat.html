Mahila E-Haat

Mahila E-Haat is an online marketing platform launched by the Ministry of Women and Child Development to promote women entrepreneurs. It allows women to showcase and sell their products/services directly to the buyers, enabling economic empowerment.

Age Limit:

No specific age limit

How to Apply:

Registration can be done online through the Mahila E-Haat official website.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhar card
  • Bank details
  • Information about the products/services being sold

Role to Apply:

Women entrepreneurs or self-help groups can apply.
