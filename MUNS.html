Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme

The Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme provides financial assistance to women entrepreneurs for setting up new ventures or expanding existing small-scale industries. It includes low-interest loans with flexible repayment options.

Age Limit:

No specific age limit, but applicants must be women entrepreneurs

How to Apply:

Applications can be submitted through Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) or partner banks offering the scheme.

Required Documents:

  • Business plan
  • Identity proof (Aadhar card)
  • Bank details
  • Financial statements (if existing business)
  • Registration documents (if applicable)

Role to Apply:

Women entrepreneurs starting small businesses such as tailoring units, parlours, or handicraft shops can apply.
