Dhanalakshmi Scheme

The scheme provides cash incentives to families with girl children to ensure education and reduce child marriages.

Age Limit:

Girl children up to 18 years

How to Apply:

Applications can be submitted through the Department of Women and Child Development, block offices, or designated centers.

Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate of the girl child
  • Aadhar card of parents
  • Bank account details
  • School enrollment certificate

Role to Apply:

Parents or guardians of girl children are eligible to apply.
