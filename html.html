Dhanalakshmi Scheme

The Dhanalakshmi Scheme provides cash incentives to families with girl children to ensure education and reduce child marriages.

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

PMEGP provides a subsidy of 15-35% on project costs to supportsmall enterprises.

Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme

The Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme provides financial assistance to women entrepreneurs for setting up new ventures or expanding existing small-scale industries.

PM Vishwakarma Yojana

PM Vishwakarma Yojana aims to support traditional artisans and craftspeople by providing financial assistance, skill training, and modern tools.

Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) Subsidy

The KVIC provides financial assistance and subsidies to set up village industries. The scheme includes margin money subsidies ranging from 25% to 35% of the project cost for general and special categories.
